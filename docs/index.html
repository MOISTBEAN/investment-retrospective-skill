The problem

The chart moves first. The story is rewritten after.

  • Price reaction mistaken for causality
  • Strong narratives treated as evidence
  • Benchmarks ignored
  • Event windows chosen after the fact
  • Missing evidence removed
  • Confidence not explicitly separated

This framework forces decomposition before interpretation.

Cases

Attribution failure modes

MRVL

Mistaking narrative shift for fundamental change

A strong reaction can precede operating confirmation.

Why this is misleading Story upgrades and fundamental change are different claims.
ASTS

Confusing validation with monetization

External validation reduces risk but does not equal revenue.

Why this is misleading Technical progress cannot substitute for unit economics.
FLNC

Confusing backlog with earnings visibility

Backlog does not guarantee timing, margin, or cash conversion.

Why this is misleading Demand visibility and earnings quality are separate variables.
